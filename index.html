Astranova

UX Case Study

Astranova is an app that enables everyday people live an Active Strong Tenacious Realistic Agile Nurturing Optimistic Vivid and All-embracing lifestyle .

PROBLEM STATEMENT

Most people are not achieving their fitness goals.

This can be people from all walks of life. Even though people report feeling healthier, stronger, and being in a better mood when they exercise, they struggle to meet their goals because of trouble finding the motivation, choosing the best activity, scheduling issues, and shortage of positive affirmation. Overall, they do want to work out more and are indeed receptive to different forms of support and motivation (social, professional, and technological).

POSSIBLE SOLUTION

Our AI Personal Trainer is named .Astranova. because our solution is about enabling everyday people to live a Active Strong Tenacious Realistic Agile Nurturing Optimistic Vivid and All-embracing lifestyle.

This AI-driven app will merge technology & psychology to provide:

  • motivational companionship
  • moral support
  • personalized training
  • goal-based coaching
  • body science resources
